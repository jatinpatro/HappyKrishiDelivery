🌿 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

HappyKrishi Delivery ("we", "us", or "our") operates the HappyKrishi farm delivery platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number — for account creation and OTP verification
  • Email address — optional, used for login OTP and notifications
  • Name and profile details — to personalise your experience
  • Delivery address — to deliver your orders
  • Order history — to process and track your deliveries
  • Device location — only when you share it during active order tracking
  • Device token (FCM) — for push notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send OTP for account verification and login
  • Send order status notifications
  • Manage your wallet and transactions
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve our service

3. Phone Verification

We use Firebase Authentication (by Google) for phone number OTP verification. By providing your phone number, you agree to receive a one-time SMS for verification purposes. Standard SMS charges may apply.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google) — for phone OTP verification
  • Our delivery team — only name and delivery address for order fulfilment
  • Payment processors — for wallet top-up transactions

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers in India. We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

6. Your Rights

You can request to view, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us. You can also delete your account from the Profile section of the app.

7. Children

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
